Dachshunds are scent hound dogs used to hunt badgers and other animals such as rabbits and foxes.

Different Types

There are three types of Dachshund Breeds. Smooth, Wirehaired, and Longhaired.

Life Span

The life span for Dachshunds is anywhere from twelve to sixteen years. The common cause of death for this breed is cancer.

Dachshund Purpose

Dachshunds were originally bred to be able to hunt for badgers. Due to their short legs and narrow body it made it easier for them to track the scent and crawl into burrows to find their prey.

The Size

This breed comes in two common sizes, standard and miniature. Standard Dachshunds weigh up to 35 pounds and minitature Dachshunds weigh up to 11 pounds.

Dachshund's Size

Dachshunds are the smallest hound.
